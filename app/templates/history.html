Our Founder

Gia Gupta, a high school senior, is the founder of mindmoves.io, a game website designed specifically for older Parkinson's patients. Recognizing that most existing games target younger audiences, Gia set out to develop a unique platform accessible on both mobile phones and the web, aimed at enhancing motor skills for those with Parkinson's.

With a passion for dance as a competitive dancer at her school, Gia is also exploring dance movement therapy as a potential benefit for Parkinson's patients. Alongside her interests in computer science and volunteer work, she has combined her skills and dedication to create this innovative resource for the Parkinson's community.
